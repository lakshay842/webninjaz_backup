SERVICES

Industrial Laser Cleaning

Here to provide environmentally preferred removal of thin coatings, residues and contamiant layers. Our industrial laser cleaning services are designed to meet the needs of a wide range of industries and applications.

We specialize in cleaning welds, removing corrosion from structures and machinery, and cleaning equipment in a variety of settings. Our state-of-the-art laser cleaning technology can also remove scale from stainless steel, as well as clean corroded equipment in warehouses.

We are committed to providing mobile service and working directly with our customers to develop customized solutions. Our laser cleaning process is relatively dust-free and minimally disruptive, making it an ideal choice for shutdowns and other time-sensitive projects. With our small footprint and specialized equipment, we can work in tight spaces without making a mess.

Let us help you extend the lifespan of your equipment and improve the overall safety and quality of your industrial operations.

Some applications where we can assist

  • NDT surface preparation
  • Paint removal from assets and equipment
  • Internal and external stainless steel cleaning
  • Shutdown services
  • Corrosion removal from assets, equipment and assets
  • Water scale and bore water stain removal
  • Laad paint removal
  • Thin surface residues and contaminant layers
